<h1><strong>Youth Festival @ South East 2026 - Where Your Future Begins! 🌟</strong></h1>
<p>Ready to level up your life? Join us for two epic days at Singapore Expo
Hall 4A where you'll discover amazing opportunities in careers, money matters,
and living your best life!</p>
<h4><strong>What's in it for you?</strong></h4>
<p>We're here to help you unlock your potential, boost your confidence, and
make moves towards your dreams.</p>
<h4><strong>Interactive Zones:</strong></h4>
<p><strong>Career:</strong> Whether you're a recent graduate, career changer,
or simply looking to level up your professional game, we've got everything
you need under one roof. Connect directly with employers at our youth-focused
job fair, expand your professional network through dedicated networking
sessions, and get personalised guidance from our career coaches. Learn
to optimise your LinkedIn profile in our hands-on workshop, discover new
skills through our upskilling sessions, and book a one-to-one career advisory
session for tailored advice that fits your unique goals. Don't miss this
opportunity to take charge of your future!&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Lifestyle:</strong> Prioritise your wellbeing with our comprehensive
workshop on stress, burnout, and sleep management designed specifically
for working individuals, and take advantage of confidential one-to-one
mental health screenings to support your mental wellness journey. Get your
body moving with energising BodyCombat sessions and relaxing yoga classes
that will leave you feeling refreshed and revitalised. Discover how to
live more sustainably through our engaging food sustainability workshop,
where you'll learn to make eco-friendly choices, and explore conscious
fashion at our fashion sustainability workshop. Connect with local community
services at booths from South East Youth Ambassadors (SE-YA) and People's
Association Youth Movement (PAYM) to discover ongoing support and programmes
in your area. This is your opportunity to invest in a healthier, more sustainable
lifestyle whilst building connections with like-minded individuals – come
and transform the way you live, work, and thrive!</p>
<p><strong>Finance: </strong>Whether you're just starting your career or
looking to make smarter money decisions, this dedicated zone offers everything
you need to build financial confidence. Explore exciting opportunities
at booths from leading organisations including DBS, Housing Development
Board, Institute of Financial Literacy, and MoneyOwl, where you can discover
career paths in finance and learn about essential services like housing
schemes and investment options. Enhance your financial knowledge through
expert-led workshops by DBS and the Institute of Financial Literacy, covering
everything from budgeting basics to investment strategies. Don't miss this
chance to connect with industry professionals, gain valuable financial
skills, and take control of your financial wellbeing – your future self
will thank you for making this smart investment in your financial literacy
journey!</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Jump between different areas focused on career growth, financial planning,
finding that perfect work-life balance. Whether you're just starting out
or already on your journey, there's something here for you.</p>
<p><strong>The Bottom Line: Come connect with career opportunities, master your finances, prioritise your wellbeing, and figure out your next moves. It's time to turn your aspirations into action!</strong>
</p>
