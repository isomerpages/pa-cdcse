<h1><strong>Youth Festival @ South East 2026</strong></h1>
<p></p>
<p><strong>We Had an Amazing Two Days at Singapore Expo Hall 4A!</strong>
</p>
<p>What an incredible turnout! Over two epic days, participants discovered
amazing opportunities in careers, money matters, and living their best
life at Singapore Expo Hall 4A.</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong>What Participants Experienced:</strong>
</p>
<p>We helped attendees unlock their potential, boost their confidence, and
make moves towards their dreams through three dynamic interactive zones.</p>
<p></p>
<p>60</p>
<p><strong>Career Zone:</strong> Whether participants were recent graduates,
career changers, or simply looking to level up their professional game,
we had everything they needed under one roof. Attendees connected directly
with employers at our youth-focused job fair, expanded their professional
networks through dedicated networking sessions, and received personalised
guidance from our career coaches. Many learned to optimise their LinkedIn
profiles in our hands-on workshops, discovered new skills through our upskilling
sessions, and booked one-to-one career advisory sessions for tailored advice
that fitted their unique goals. It was fantastic to see so many people
taking charge of their futures!</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong>Lifestyle Zone:</strong> Participants prioritised their wellbeing
through our comprehensive workshops on stress, burnout, and sleep management
designed specifically for working individuals, and many took advantage
of confidential one-to-one mental health screenings to support their mental
wellness journeys. Attendees got their bodies moving with energising BodyCombat
sessions and relaxing yoga classes that left them feeling refreshed and
revitalised. Many discovered how to live more sustainably through our engaging
food sustainability workshop, learning to make eco-friendly choices, and
explored conscious fashion at our fashion sustainability workshop. Participants
connected with local community services at booths from South East Youth
Ambassadors (SE-YA) and People's Association Youth Movement (PAYM), discovering
ongoing support and programmes in their areas. It was wonderful to see
people investing in healthier, more sustainable lifestyles whilst building
connections with like-minded individuals!</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong>Finance Zone:</strong> Whether participants were just starting
their careers or looking to make smarter money decisions, this dedicated
zone offered everything they needed to build financial confidence. Attendees
explored exciting opportunities at booths from leading organisations including
POSB, Housing Development Board, Institute of Financial Literacy, and MoneyOwl,
where they discovered career paths in finance and learned about essential
services like housing schemes and investment options. Many enhanced their
financial knowledge through expert-led workshops by DBS and the Institute
of Financial Literacy, covering everything from budgeting basics to investment
strategies. Participants connected with industry professionals, gained
valuable financial skills, and took control of their financial wellbeing!</p>
<p><strong>The Results:</strong> Participants successfully connected with
career opportunities, mastered their finances, prioritised their wellbeing,
and figured out their next moves. It was inspiring to see so many people
turn their aspirations into action over these two incredible days!</p>
