<h3><strong>Building a Network of Care for Our Community</strong></h3>
<p>Launched in 2025, Club South East 100 is a donation membership programme
connecting community-minded individuals and organisations to provide sustained
support for vulnerable residents in our district through monthly $100 donations
or one-time contributions.</p>
<p>Your generous donation will help these residents overcome challenges caused
by rising cost of living, through financial relief programmes that support
their immediate family needs. Let us work together towards helping our
vulnerable residents lead dignified lives.</p>
<h4><strong>How Your $100 Monthly Donations Transforms Lives</strong></h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Provides 20 meals a month's for a low-income resident</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provides one month worth of supermarket vouchers for a senior living alone</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Offsets one month of Service and Conservancy Charges (S&amp;CC) or utilities
arrears for a vulnerable family</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Uplifts a disadvantage child/youth through two sessions of developmental
and mentorship programme</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Why Join Club South East 100</strong></h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>With every donation, receive 250% tax deductions in your next Year of
Assessment.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>See how your contributions transform lives through impact and annual reports
that recounts meaningful differences in vulnerable families across our
community.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Receive invitations to exclusive members-only events such as visits to
the parliamentary house and launches of new collaborations with partners</p>
</li>
</ul>
