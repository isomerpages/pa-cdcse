<h1>SEEDS of Wellness</h1>
<p>In 2022, The Straits Times reported that the number of suicides in Singapore
had risen by 25.9%, a total of 476 suicides, increased from the 378 suicides
reported in 2021. The report added that the suicide cases spread across
most age groups but were more prevalent amongst the youth and elderly.</p>
<p>In response to the pressing concern of mental health issues in our community,
we had initiated the SEEDS of Wellness programme to complement the nationwide
efforts in raising awareness and destigmatising mental health issues.</p>

<p><strong>a. Community Mental Wellness Grant</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>We welcome community partners to customise mental wellness-related programmes
to their specific needs. We encourage our partners to identify the needs
within your community and propose engaging activities based on well-researched
evidence for your organisations.</p>
<p>Capped at $10,000 per project per application.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>b. Musical and Skit</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 60%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="12 Going on 13 Musical Skit" src="/images/What We Do/Health/12_13_SECDC_A4_ART_Comm_Skit_raw.jpg">
</div>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>The musical and skit were curated to raise awareness and promote a deeper
understanding of mental well-being issues through relatable characters
who overcome adversity. Additionally, they aim to alleviate feelings of
loneliness often associated with mental well-being challenges.</p>
<p></p>
</li>
<li>
<p>We've wrapped up '12 Going on 13', our SEEDS of Wellness initiative that
reached out to over 2300 participants in the South East District through
theatre and community events.</p>

<p><strong>c. Self-Help Toolkit</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Attendees of the musical and skit will receive a self-help toolkit, including
stress-relief tools and helplines to reach out to when facing mental health
issues.</p>
<p></p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The toolkit will also feature additional resources for mental health strategies
and stress-coping techniques.</p>
</li>
</ul>
