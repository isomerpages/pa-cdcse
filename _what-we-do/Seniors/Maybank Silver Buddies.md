<h1><strong>Maybank SG60 Silver Buddies Programme - Banking on Friendship, Giving Back to the Community</strong></h1>
<p></p>
<p>When Maybank Singapore reached 65 years of serving the community, they
celebrated with heart, not fanfare. The Maybank SG60 Silver Buddies @ South
East became more than corporate social responsibility – it was a partnership
with South East CDC that wove Maybank's resources into local community
life.</p>
<p></p>
<p>With Maybank's funding, the programme transforms corporate volunteering
into authentic community connections through direct, hands-on engagement
with residents. It is corporate responsibility reimagined – where giving
becomes partnership and impact becomes lasting transformation.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="On stage SSA's token of appreciation" src="/images/What We Do/Seniors/silver_buddies_01.jpg">
</div>
<p>Since July 2025, something beautiful has been unfolding across the South
East District as Maybank volunteers rolled up their sleeves and opened
their hearts, transforming ordinary moments into extraordinary connections.
Through partnerships with 4S Active Ageing Centre @ Eunos Crescent, The
Salvation Army Peacehaven Bedok Arena, Thye Hua Kwan Active Ageing Centre
@ MacPherson, Care Community Services Society, Helping Joy, and Thye Hua
Kwan Family Service Centre @ MacPherson, the programme weaves together
communal dining sessions filled with shared laughter, gentle assistance
during grocery shopping trips, festive celebrations that light up faces,
care packs that deliver warmth beyond their contents and home cleaning
services that sweep away not just dust, but loneliness too. It's not just
about providing essentials or improving living conditions – though this
matter deeply – it's about the families whose homes become a sanctuary
again after volunteers restore their comfort, creating a community where
no one walks alone and care flows as naturally as conversations over a
shared meal.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Whether you're a corporate partner looking to make a difference, a volunteer
ready to connect hearts, or a Social Service Agency (SSA), seeking collaboration
opportunities – there's a place for you in our growing community.</p>
