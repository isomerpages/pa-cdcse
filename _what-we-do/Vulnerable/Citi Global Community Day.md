<h1>Citi Global Community Day – Global Hearts, Community Impact</h1>
<p>South East CDC has partnered with Citi Singapore on the month-long Citi
Global Community Day (GCD) campaign, exemplifying Citi's ongoing commitment
to supporting those in need and strengthening bonds in the community.</p>
<h4><strong>Growing Impact, Strengthening Bonds</strong></h4>
<p>The partnership has shown remarkable growth since it started in June 2024
where over 230 Citi volunteers reached out to more than 600 beneficiaries
across 13 Social Service Agencies (SSA). From accompanying elderly residents
on meaningful outings to providing essential house cleaning assistance,
and creating memories during children’s excursions, these volunteers showed
how small acts can create lasting impact and bonds.</p>
<p>In 2025, the partnership had reached new heights with more than 480 Citi
volunteers stepping forward to deliver practical assistance, companionship,
and joy to over 1,200 beneficiaries across 19 SPEs throughout the South
East District. This doubling of both volunteer participation and beneficiary
reach isn’t just about numbers – it’s about twice as many hearts connecting,
and twice as many lives being transformed.</p>
<p>We look forward to the continued impact and stories of our network of
partners in 2026.</p>
