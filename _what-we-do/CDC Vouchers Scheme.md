# About CDC Vouchers Scheme 2026


As announced in Budget 2025, all Singaporean households will receive an additional $800 in CDC Vouchers to help with daily expenses. The vouchers are distributed in two tranches — $500 in May 2025 and $300 in January 2026.

Starting 2 January 2026, you can visit [go.gov.sg/cdcv](https://go.gov.sg/cdcv) to claim the $300 CDC Vouchers 2026 (January). The process for claiming and spending the vouchers remains the same.

The CDC Vouchers Scheme 2026 (January) is valid till 31 December 2026.

[Learn](https://vouchers.cdc.gov.sg/residents/info/) how you can claim your CDC Vouchers 2026 (January).
