<h2><strong>Jobs Nearby @ South East - Find Work Close to Home</strong></h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Jobs Nearby @ CDC" src="/images/Events/jn.png">
</div>
<h4><strong>Looking for a job that fits your life? We're here to help.</strong></h4>
<p>Whether you're caring for family, starting your career, or looking for
something flexible, Jobs Nearby @ South East connects you with employment
opportunities right in your neighbourhood.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h4><strong>Three Types of Jobs to Match Your Needs</strong></h4>
<p><strong>Local Jobs with flexible arrangement - </strong>Work close to
home either full-time, part-time, freelance, or flexible hours that fits
your schedule. Perfect if you're new to job searching or need flexibility.</p>
<p><strong>Retail and Service Jobs</strong> - Positions at shops, cafes, and
businesses in your area with straightforward applications. Great for steady
work without complicated interviews.</p>
<p><strong>Professional Careers</strong> - Office jobs and career opportunities
with full support, including connections to specialist employment agencies
when needed.</p>
<h4><strong>Why Choose Jobs Nearby?</strong></h4>
<p>Living in South East means you're close to Changi Airport, Changi Business
Park, and dozens of markets and coffeeshops - all offering different types
of work. We know the area, and recognise that everyone's situation and
needs are different.</p>
<p>Maybe you're in Bedok caring for elderly parents and need work nearby.
Perhaps you're a professional in Eunos ready for your next career move.
Or you might be looking for flexible hours to supplement your family's
income. Whatever your situation, we'll match you with the right opportunity.</p>
<h4><strong>We Understand Our Community</strong></h4>
<p>South East is home to, seniors, young people, and families from all walks
of life. This diversity is our strength - experienced residents often mentor
newcomers, and we create connections that benefit everyone.</p>
<h4><strong>Ready to Start?</strong></h4>
<p>Finding the right job shouldn't be stressful. Our team knows the local
job market and will work with you personally to find opportunities that
suit your skills, location, and lifestyle.</p>
